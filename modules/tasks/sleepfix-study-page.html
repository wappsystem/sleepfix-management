SleepFix Study Page

Welcome to the SleepFix study. Begin your journey here to improve your sleep and daytime functioning.

How to Start
Step 1: Watch our short videos about the therapy

This will help you understand how the therapy and app works. Click here to watch these videos. For more information about the therapy and the study, please refer to the Therapy Guide.

Step 2: Find out how the app works

Next, learn how SleepFix delivers your personalised sleep treatment. Click here to watch our short video ‘How to use SleepFix’ for instructions and read the Setting up SleepFix manual.

Step 3: Complete your questionnaires

You will have received an email from the SleepFix team inviting you to complete your first questionnaire. There are a series of questionnaires to fill out over the course of and following the therapy – they will help us track your progress. Once the first questionnaire is complete, you will receive an email within a few days giving you access to the app.

Step 4: Download SleepFix

Follow the instructions in the email to download the SleepFix app which includes your unique access code. It is important that the code is entered because it provides access to the full version of the app.

Step 5: Begin SleepFix

You’re ready to begin the sleep program. Remember to stick to your new sleep times and check in with SleepFix every day.

The app is in its early development stage and you may still experience some technical difficulties. Refer to our Bugs and known issues document for some common app issues workarounds. If there are any other concerns, please email us.

Any more questions? Have a look at our Frequently Asked Questions. If you can’t find what you are looking for or need more help, contact us on:
